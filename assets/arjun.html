ARJUN


The idea is to recreate a new vehicle which encapsulates the traits of any modern tank that are maneuverability, robustness, strength and lethal firing power


Objective

To build a manually controlled robot capable of traversing over irregular terrains, shooting down a target and finish the course.

The Game

1) Traverse over different terrains and cover checkpoints.

2) Shoot the target by a canon in minimum number of tries.
