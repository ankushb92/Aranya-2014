INCLINO


The objective is to design a tower like structure that is inclined at some angle with the vertical and is strong enough to hold a weight without breaking or falling.


Judging

There are three parameters for judging:

  1. Deadweight of the structure: It should be as small as possible.
  2. Distance between the projections of the topmost corner of the structure before and after placing the weight.
  3. Angle of inclination with the vertical. It should be at least 5 degrees.

Certificates and Prizes

  1. Certificates of Excellence will be awarded to the winning team.
  2. Prizes for this event will be worth Rs. 5000.
