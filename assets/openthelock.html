OPEN THE LOCK


To open a three digit lock on the basis of three numbers.


Rules

In this event a team has to open a three digit lock on the basis of three numbers.
A team can get these three numbers after doing three designed tasks.
For example, to make a given pattern of led's on a breadboard and so on.

  • There are three barriers to reach the lock. At each barrier a team has to solve a logical problem.
  • After performing task at the barrier a numeric is given to them. After crossing three barriers, a team has three numbers.
  • Now, team has to open the lock by making permutation of the three numbers they get at three barriers.
  • The team who performs in the least time is the winning team.

Prizes

Certificates of Excellence will be awarded to the winning team.
