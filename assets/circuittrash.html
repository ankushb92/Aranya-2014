CIRCUIT TRASH


To design a given circuit out of the components in the trash. Team who makes the correct circuit in the least time is the winning team.


Rules

  • A common trash consisting of wanted and unwanted components is provided to all the teams.
  • A circuit on paper is provided to each team which they have to build.
  • Now each team will find out all their required components from the trash.
  • After finding components the team has to build the given circuit on breadboard.
  • The team which built the correct circuit in the least time is the winning team.

Prizes

Certificates of Excellence will be awarded to the winning team.
